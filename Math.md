## _Sum of Series_ 

---
<br>


>### 1+2+3+...k

<br>

$\sum_{n=1}^{k} (\frac{n(n+1)}{2})$

<br>
<br>

>### $1^2+2^2+3^2+...k^2$

<br>

$\sum_{n=1}^k (\frac{n(n+1)(2n+1)}{6})$
